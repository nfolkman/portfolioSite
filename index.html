Work With Me

As a software engineer and web developer my goal is to help deliver projects in the most efficient way possible, and to do so by utilizing effective problem solving and communication skills.

To hit the ground running, here's a brief overview of the project development process:

Consultation

The goal of a consultation is to make sure we are on the same page from the get-go. This project is important to you, and understanding why and how I can help is important to me. A team with a gameplan to get this project developed and delivered right!

Proposal

After the initial consultation, I will put together a personalized proposal and deliver it to you within 1-2 business days. Inside this proposal will be: expected project scope, approximated timeline, pricing, etc. Along with the proposal, a project contract will be shared to officially get us up and running in tandem.

Development

We're to the development stage! This is where fingers meet keys and the web app/site development process officially begins. Designs are shared, code is written, and consistent communication happens throughout the lead-up to launch.

Launch

This is the day we've all been waiting for. The day this project-investment comes to fruition. After final testing and compensation, the product is yours. Unless otherwise specified during the proposal stage, this is where the project leaves my hands forever and goes out into the world with you. Perhaps an emotional day, but a meaningful one.
